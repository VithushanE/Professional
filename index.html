About Me

Hello there! My name is Vithushan Esan and I am an experienced financial professional. I graduated from the Lazaridis School of Business and Economics at Wilfrid Laurier University, with a major in accounting and a minor in economics. Throughout my University journey, I learned a lot of important financial knowledge and I had experience in working in a variety of public sector positions.


After graduating, I decided to transition to the private sector and worked in two financial institutions. During this time, I further enhanced my financial knowledge and was exposed to crucial investment and wealth building tools. While working at these companies, I realized the importance of coding and I wanted to learn and become fluent in several coding languges. Since then, I applied to the University of Toronto's Full Stack Flex Coding Bootcamp and I am motivated to learn this knowledge. Upon graduation, my aim is to return to either the public sector or financial industry to further develop my skills and career. Please feel free to look through my website to see a portfolio of my past coding work and for ways us to connect!
